About me

Not my first rodeo

Before I went back to school for Website Design and Development at Full Sail, I was a Project Manager / Mechanical Engineer for weapons development programs at Sandia National Laboratories. Even though I worked with some amazing people and the technology was interesting, I knew it wasn't the right field for me.

So I saved up money in order to take a break, find myself, and have the opportunity to try new things. Along the way I re-discovered my love of art, graphic design, data and coding. In the summer of 2014, I enrolled at Full Sail. All my courses at Full Sail challenged me to get back in touch with my creative side and I found balance again between my left side and right side of my brain.

Life away from the computer

I truly believe in having a healthy work life balance. My hobbies include running, cooking, reading, and spending time with my friends and family. I've ran countless marathons, half-marathons and 5Ks. I make a mean green chili mac 'n cheese. There's always a book on my nightstand.

Additionally, I have four of the best cats in the world and am not ashamed to be called a crazy cat lady.
